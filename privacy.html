Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2026

1. Introduction

WellsPlusDaily ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website wellsplusdaily.com and provides creator-brand partnership brokerage services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you interact with our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, company name, and any information you include in an inquiry or correspondence. We do not collect payment card information directly — all payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc., a third-party payment processor.

We may also collect standard technical information automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and referring URLs, for operational and security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, evaluate partnership fit, communicate regarding active engagements, process payments through our payment processor, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Payment Processing

All financial transactions are processed securely by Stripe, Inc. When you make a payment, you are subject to Stripe's privacy policy and terms of service in addition to ours. We do not store full payment card details on our servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, maintain business records, and comply with applicable law. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us directly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at donj@wellsplusdaily.com.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Services

Our services may involve third-party tools for contract execution (PandaDoc) and payment processing (Stripe). Each third party has its own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at donj@wellsplusdaily.com.
