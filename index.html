About

A firm built for
high-value partnerships.

WellsPlusDaily is a tech-enabled brokerage that structures, negotiates, and closes sponsorship deals between brands and creators. We don't operate a marketplace — every engagement is handled directly, with a dedicated point of contact managing the full deal lifecycle.

We work with brands across technology, e-commerce, finance, and consumer goods, matching them to creators whose audience profile, content quality, and commercial track record meet our standards. Deals are not facilitated on volume. Each engagement is evaluated and structured individually.

Our process covers everything from initial creator identification and outreach, through rate negotiation and contract execution, to payment processing and campaign delivery. Both parties receive structured documentation at every stage.

Brand Partnerships

Matching, outreach & deal structure

We evaluate your brand category, target audience, and campaign objectives. Our team conducts direct outreach to highly-vetted creators to secure the perfect match. Every engagement is structured individually to guarantee alignment and performance.

Creator Campaigns

Roster curation & rate negotiation

We represent a curated roster of creators who meet strict audience and content standards. Our dedicated managers handle all rate negotiations to secure fair, market-adjusted pricing. We ensure both sides arrive at the table informed and ready to execute.

Contract Execution

Digital signing & documented terms

Ambiguity is the enemy of execution. We issue comprehensive digital contracts to lock in the Technical Brief before any work begins. Every deliverable, timeline, and compliance requirement is legally bound.

Financial Operations

Secure milestones & payout routing

Creator compensation is strictly tied to validated delivery metrics. We manage the 80/20 payout structure, releasing initial funds upon delivery and holding the remainder for compliance validation. All payments are routed securely through regulated financial infrastructure.

Escalation & Tracking

Delivery tracking & brand compliance

Human curation alone cannot scale. We ingest public metadata via social APIs to continuously verify timeline adherence and FTC disclosure visibility. Any deviations are immediately routed through our highly structured escalation map for rapid resolution.
How It Works

A structured process,
start to finish.

Every engagement follows a defined sequence. There are no cold introductions, no ambiguous handoffs. We manage the full lifecycle so both parties arrive at each stage informed and prepared.

01

Intake & Matching

We assess your brand category, campaign objectives, and target audience profile. Creators are identified from our roster based on audience alignment, content category, and commercial track record — not surface-level niche overlap.

02

Outreach & Negotiation

We conduct direct outreach to selected creators on your behalf, present the campaign scope, and manage rate negotiation through to agreed terms. You receive a curated shortlist with proposed structures before any commitment is made.

03

Contracts & Delivery

Once terms are confirmed, we issue digital contracts through a structured signing process and coordinate payment milestones. We remain the point of contact through campaign delivery, final review, and sign-off.
