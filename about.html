Our Thesis

The creator economy suffers from a fundamental misalignment of incentives. For brands (Demand), creators represent an inherently untrusted supply side with unquantifiable execution risk. For creators (Supply), brands are slow, highly bureaucratic, and often adversarial regarding payments.

We recognized that the core friction in modern commercial partnerships is not discovery or matching. It is execution risk. At a deal size of $5,000 to $15,000, neither the brand nor the creator has the economic leverage to credibly enforce the contract on the other party in the event of a breach.

Wells+Daily was built to solve this exact problem. We do not operate as an advertising agency, a passive matching marketplace, or a talent management firm. We operate exclusively as an actuarial enforcement layer. We monetize the function of credibly enforcing delivery and compliance, sitting squarely in the middle of every transaction.

The Dual-Contract Framework

To remove execution risk, we had to architect an entirely new structural paradigm: the dual-contract execution framework. Brands do not directly employ creators; they contract with the Brokerage to access our curated supply and utilize our Bounded Guarantee. Creators contract with the Brokerage to access Deal Frameworks and guarantee their payments through our Escrow systems.

The Bounded Guarantee

Brands receive a 100% credit-and-replacement guarantee bounded by a strict 90-day execution window. If a creator breaches the Technical Brief, timeline, or compliance specifications, the Brokerage replaces the supply at zero additional cost to the brand.

The 80/20 Payout Structure

Creator compensation is strictly tied to validated delivery metrics. 80% of the net fee is released upon initial validated delivery. The remaining 20% is held in a 30-day compliance validation hold to secure the brand against post-delivery non-compliance, such as unauthorized asset deletion or retroactive FTC disclosure removal.

The Trust Stack

Human curation alone cannot scale to meet the enforcement demands of enterprise brands. We built the Trust Stack—a proprietary suite of software and API integrations—to programmatically monitor and enforce every engagement.

  • Contract Execution APIs Automated generation, digital signing, and secure storage of modular Deal Frameworks, ensuring every technical brief is legally locked before execution begins.
  • Algorithmic Compliance Monitoring Ingesting public metadata via official social APIs (Meta, Google, TikTok) to continuously verify timeline adherence, talking point inclusion, and FTC disclosure visibility during the 30-day validation hold.
  • The Escalation Map A highly structured dispute resolution path. Initial compliance claims are processed immediately via our always-on AI support infrastructure, referencing the locked Technical Brief. Subjective disputes or complex structural remediations are escalated to human operators within strict SLAs.

Brokerage Governance Doctrine

We operate on a set of absolute principles that govern how we evaluate risk, manage our curated roster, and enforce our contracts. We do not compromise on these doctrines, regardless of deal size.

Quality Over Growth
The Bounded Guarantee requires highly reliable supply. We will constrain the size of our creator roster to protect the integrity of the guarantee. The economic survivability of our business depends on extreme curation, not unchecked marketplace expansion.

Institutional Visibility
Brands and Creators must have absolute transparency into the state of their contracts. There are no black boxes. Our Escrow dashboards provide real-time updates on validation holds, payment routing, and escalation status.

Absolute Enforcement Legibility
Every rule, penalty, and payout trigger must be explicitly documented and understandable to both sides before a Deal Framework is signed. Ambiguity is the enemy of enforcement.
